What is a hero? Are they someone who helps others? Do they stand up for what is right? An everyday person can be heroic some people are more heroic than others. They have lasting impacts on our world to this day. These people possess the bravery and smarts to change the world forever. Alexander Hamilton and Benjamin Franklin's perseverance and bravery when overcoming life's difficulties to stand up for what they thought was right; made them heroes.

Alexander Hamilton had great perseverance working his way to the top. Alexander was an orphaned boy who grew up on a poor island with big goals. He had to work for a living before his teens. After the first battles of the revolution, “Hamilton left college to join the continental army serving under George Washington” (Scholastic) At a very young age, Hamilton joined the army. By doing this he put his life on the line to help his country. His putting his life on the line showed heroism because he risked himself to help others.  Hamilton impacted others by creating the currency we use today and our national bank. After George Washington was elected president he chose Hamilton to be the first U.S. Secretary of the Treasury. In this position “Hamilton created the first national bank and our national currency.”  (Scholastic) Hamilton impacted many people by creating our national currency. He made the first national bank. Also, he became the secretary of treasury. Hamilton proved his determination by climbing from a poor boy to a founding father.

Benjamin Franklin like Hamilton worked to make his country a better place. Benjamin Franklin was born in Boston, Massachusetts. He had 16 siblings and only went to school for two years.  He left at 10 to work at his father's shop. During the revolution, Benjamin spent his time in France “where he convinced the French king to support the colonists in their fight for independence.” (Scholastic) This shows heroism because he worked to help the people of the colonies. Also, he helped save many lives. And he helped the colonists win the war. Benjamin used his funds and worked hard to help others. Over the next many years, “Franklin worked to improve life in Philadelphia. He started a free public school and the first hospital in the Colonies. This shows impact because he improved many people's lives in the colonies. He impacted others by helping others learn. Also, by keeping them healthy. Benjamin and Hamilton both worked hard to make the colonies and our country a better place.

        Alexander Hamilton and Benjamin Franklin's determination and courage when prevailing over life's troubles while standing up for what is right shows they are true heroes. Alexander who helped win the war and helped create our economy. Also, Benjamin created the first public school and hospital in the colonies and convinced the French king to support the colonies.
